Caenorhabditis sp. 29 is a gonochoristic species belonging to the Elegans supergroup [1]. The type isolate, QG704, was collected on Barro Colorda Island, Panama by Matt Rockman. See below for more information.

Species and strain

C. nouraguensis is the most closely related species.

Raw data

The raw data are not yet available.

Assembly

The genome of C. sp. 29 QG2080 was assembled by Luke Noble (Rockman Lab) using a combination of short-read and mate-pair data.

Caenorhabditis afra (formerly sp. 7) is a gonochoristic species belonging to the Elegans supergroup. The sequenced strain, JU1286, is an inbred strain generated from the type isolate JU1199, which was isolated in Begoro, Ghana [1]. See below for more information.

Species and strain

C. afra (sp. 7) was formally described in [1]. Discussion about morphological characters can be found in [2] (termed sp. 7 here). C. imperialis is the most closely related species [1].

Zoobank entry: E705B72C-1E5D-469B-BC6A-9E539B3FDD8D

CGC strain entry: JU1286

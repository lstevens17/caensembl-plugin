Caenorhabditis latens (formerly sp. 23) is a gonochoristic species belonging to the Elegans supergroup. See below for more information.

Species and strain

C. latens (sp. 23) was formally described in [1]. C. remanei is the most closely related species [1].

Zoobank entry: C046BE47-3E23-4A7D-A760-6E1670BA3838

Raw data

The raw data can be downloaded from either ENA or NCBI (please note the raw data contain sequencing adapters and bacterial contamination - contact lewis.stevens@ed.ac.uk for “cleaned” reads):

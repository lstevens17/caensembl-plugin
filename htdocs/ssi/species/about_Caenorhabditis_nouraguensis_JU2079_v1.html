Caenorhabditis nouragensis (formerly sp. 17) is a gonochoristic species belonging to the Elegans supergroup. The sequenced strain, JU2079, is an inbred strain generated from the type isolate JU1825, which was isolated in Nouragues Forest, French Guiana [1]. See below for more information.

Species and strain

C. nouraguensis (sp. 17) was formally described in [1]. Discussion about morphological characters can be found in [2] (termed sp. 17 here). C. macrosperma and C. yunquensis are the most closely related species [1].

Caenorhabditis castelli (formally sp. 12) is a gonochoristic species belonging to the Drosophilae supergroup. The sequenced strain, JU1956, is an inbred strain generated from the type isolate JU1427, which was isolated in Nouragues, French Guiana. See below for more information.

Species/Strain Info

C. castelli (sp. 12) was formally described by Felix et. al. (2014). Discussion about morphological characters can be found in Kiontke et. al. (2011) (termed sp. 12 here). C. angaria is the most closely related species.

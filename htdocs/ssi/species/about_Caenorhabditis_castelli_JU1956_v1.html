Caenorhabditis castelli (formally sp. 12) is a gonochoristic species belonging to the Drosophilae supergroup. The sequenced strain, JU1956, is an inbred strain generated from the type isolate JU1427, which was isolated in Nouragues, French Guiana [1]. See below for more information.

Species and strain

C. castelli (sp. 12) was formally described in [1]. Discussion about morphological characters can be found in [2] (termed sp. 12 here). C. angaria is the most closely related species [1].

Zoobank entry: 31D1A176-8517-42A5-94C9-2A6F3EC7E710

CGC strain entry: JU1956
